Bulking Note

1.Sleep At least 7-8 hours, because muscles grow while you sleep, not while you exercise

2.Calories Start by adding 300-500 calories above your daily requirement

3.Rest 90 seconds to 2 minutes between large sets

Cutting schedule plan
Meal Example Benefits Time
Breakfast 4 Egg Whites + 1 Whole Egg + Spinach High protein with low calories to keep you full and preserve muscle 7:00 - 8:30 am

Dinner 200g Grilled Fish or Tuna + Steamed Broccoli Light, high-protein meal that is easy to digest before sleep. 7:30 - 9:00 PM

Drying Note

1.Cardio Add 20-40 minutes of cardio (brisk walking or cycling) after your workout 3-5 times per week

2.Fiber Focus on leafy greens to feel full longer

3.Water Drink plenty of water (3-4 liters per day) to boost metabolism and control appetite
